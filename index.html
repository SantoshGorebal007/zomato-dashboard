Zomato Power BI Dashboard

Project Overview

+

This Zomato data analysis project leverages AWS/Azure ETL/ETP pipelines to provide actionable insights across sales, operations, and customer behavior, presented through Power BI dashboards covering overall performance, KPIs, user trends, and city-specific metrics.

Overview Report

+

The Overview Page serves as the high-level executive summary of Zomato's overall business performance. It provides a quick snapshot of key business health indicators, allowing stakeholders to grasp the current state and identify immediate trends. Key Metrics include Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, Number of Active Customers, and overall Operational Efficiency Score. Visualizations typically include high-level trend lines for revenue and orders over time, geographical heatmaps for overall performance, and summary cards for current key figures. Its purpose is to offer a concise, top-down view of the business, enabling rapid assessment of performance and strategic alignmen
